# Release Notes

## Version 1.0.0 – First Production Release for Android

**Release date:** February 2026

This is the first production release of **Morning and Evening** for Android (Google Play).

### What's in this release

- **Daily devotions** – Spurgeon’s Morning and Evening devotional, one reading per day for morning and one for evening.
- **Automatic date and time** – The app opens to today’s reading. Morning devotions are shown from **2:00 AM–1:59 PM** local time; evening devotions from **2:00 PM–1:59 AM**.
- **Simple navigation** – Swipe left/right to move between days; your place is saved as you browse.
- **Readable design** – Inter font, clear layout, and support for light and dark system theme.
- **No account or internet required** – All content is included in the app; no login or data connection needed to read.

### Technical

- Text-only release (no audio).
- Supports Android 5.0 (API 21) and later.
- App size ~20 MB (App Bundle).
